## Ingredients

- 1 clove garlic, finely minced
- 2 Sweet Peppers, diced
- 2 T Olive Oil
- Salt
- 1 16oz can Lentils
- 3/4 c Vegetable Stock

## Directions

- In a wide bottomed saucepan, heat oil over medium low heat until shimmering.
- Add diced peppers and sautee 3-5 minutes or until tender.
- Add garlic and stir until fragrant, then add lentils, vegetable stock, and salt to taste.
- Simmer until roughly half of the liquid evaporates. Serve with rice.
