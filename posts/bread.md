## Ingredients

- 3 c Flour
- 1 t Active Yeast
- 3 t Salt

## Directions

- Mix inredients in a large bowl. Add 1 1/2 c water and stir until a shaggy dough forms.
- Cover and leave at room temperature until dough doubles in size, usually at least 8 hours but it can sit for up to 24.
- Turn dough out and lightly knead on a floured surface. Form into desired shape or shapes and cover on a piece of parchment paper for 30 minutes.
- Preheat over to 500 degrees. Put your dutch over or other baking vessel into the oven while it preheats.
- Cut a small slit into your dough with a paring knife and leave uncovered for 10 more minutes.
- Drop your parchment paper into the Dutch Oven. Spray your Dutch Oven and dough with water before covering.
- Bake for 25 minutes, then remove cover, spray with water again, and bake for another 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
- Allow your bread to cool completely before cutting, at least an hour.
