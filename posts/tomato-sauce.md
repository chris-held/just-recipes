## Ingredients

- 4-6 cloves garlic, finely minced
- 1/4 c Olive Oil
- 1 28 oz can Crushed or Diced Tomatoes
- 1 T Tomato Paste
- 2 T Butter
- 1 Carrot
- Oregano
- Salt

## Directions

- In a large pot heat oil over medium heat and add diced garlic. Cook for 1-2 minutes or until fragrant
- Add tomato paste and oregano and stir until combined.
- Add canned tomatoes and carrot, cover and reduce heat to a low simmer.
- Leave covered for at least an hour, stirring occasionally.
- Add butter and salt to taste. If you prefer chunkier suace this is good as is, or you can blend it. If you decide to blend it pulse at 1 second intervals and let it come to a stop between pulses. This will keep your sauce from turning orange.
