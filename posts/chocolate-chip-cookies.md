## Ingredients

- 2 2/3 c Flour
- 1 T Salt
- 1/4 t Baking Soda
- 2 Sticks Unsalted Butter
- 1/2 c Sugar
- 1 1/2 c Brown Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 c Chocolate Chips

## Directions

- In a medium saucepan, heat butter over medium low heat until it starts to barely turn brown, then remove from heat and refrigerate until solid, about one hour.
- Combine Flour, Salt and Baking Soda in a medium mixing bowl.
- Add butter, sugar and brown sugar into a stand mixer and mix at medium speed for 3 minutes or until combined.
- Add half of flour mixture and mix to combine, then add the other half. Don't overmix your cookies or they'll be too chewy.
- Add chocolate chips and fold in with a spatula. The 1 c is more of a guideline, add more or less depending on how chocolatey you like your cookies.
- Roll into a ball about the size of a golf ball and arrange on a nonstick baking sheet.
- Bake in a 350 degree oven for 10-15 minutes.
