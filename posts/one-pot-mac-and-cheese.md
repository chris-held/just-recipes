## Ingredients

- 8 oz Pasta
- 1 1/2 c Whole Milk
- 8 oz Shredded Cheese

## Directions

- Add Milk and 1 c water to a medium saucepan and bring to a boil
- Add Pasta and cook for 6-8 minutes. It shouldn't be fully cooked at this point, but pretty close
- Stir in Shredded Cheese. The type of cheese you add here will make a big difference on texture. Adding 4 oz american cheese and then 4 oz cheddar will give the smoothest texture, but really any will do. Avoid using pre shredded cheese if you can, it makes the mac and cheese clumpy.
- Let sit off heat, covered, for 3-5 minutes before serving.
