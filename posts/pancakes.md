## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 c Flour
- 1 t Salt
- 3 t Baking Powder
- 1/2 t Baking Soda
- 3 T Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 1/2 C Milk
- 1/4 c Oil
- 1 T Butter

## Directions

- Combine dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
- Combine wet ingredients into a smaller mixing bowl.
- Add wet mixture to dry mixture and lightly fold together. Flour streaks are okay, you don't want to overmix.
- Melt butter in a large saucepan and make one test pancake to soak up the excess and give to your dog.
