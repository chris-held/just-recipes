date: "7/17/2020"

---

## Ingredients

- 2 Medium Sweet Potatoes
- 6 T Butter
- 3/4 c Water
- Salt & Pepper

## Directions

- Peel sweet potatoes and dice into roughly 1/4 inch chunks.
- Add water, butter, and sweet potatoes to large pot or dutch oven and simmer, covered, until potatoes break apart easily, 30 minutes to an hour based on how small you cut your pieces.
- Add everything to a blender or food processor and pulse until well blended. Add salt and pepper to taste.
