## Ingredients

- 2 T Butter
- 2 Jasmine Rice
- 3 c Vegetable Stock
- 1/4 tsp Cumin
- Salt

## Directions

- Melt the butter in a medium sauce pot and stir in cumin.
- Add rice and cook for 1-2 minutes.
- Add Vegetable Stock and cover on medium low heat for 20 minutes
- Remove from heat and leave, covered, for 5-10 minutes. Add salt to taste.
