## Ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
- 1 lb fresh broccoli florets
- 2 T Olive Oil
- Salt

## Directions

- In a wide bottomed saucepan, heat oil over medium heat until shimmering.
- Add garlic and heat for 30 seconds or until very fragrant, then increase heat to high and add broccoli and 1 c water.
- Cover for 5-10 minutes, stirring ocasionally. Salt to taste. Eat immediately, or it can be stored for 7-10 days.
